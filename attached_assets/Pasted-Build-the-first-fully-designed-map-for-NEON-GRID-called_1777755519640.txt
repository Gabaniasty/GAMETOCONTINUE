Build the first fully designed map for NEON GRID called "TERMINAL".
It is a medium-sized indoor cyberpunk facility — corridors, open halls, 
catwalks, and chokepoints. Good for AWP quick-scoping and mid-range duels.
Everything built in Three.js. No external files required.

── TERMINAL MAP LAYOUT (top-down, 100x100 units) ──

                    NORTH SPAWN (Team A)
    ┌──────────────────────────────────────────┐
    │  [SERVER ROOM]  [CATWALK]  [CONTROL HUB] │
    │        │            │            │        │
    │  [CORRIDOR A]  [MAIN HALL]  [CORRIDOR B]  │
    │        │            │            │        │
    │  [SIDE ROOM]   [CHOKE]    [GENERATOR]    │
    └──────────────────────────────────────────┘
                    SOUTH SPAWN (Team B)

── Create public/js/maps/TerminalMap.js ──

All geometry uses these shared materials (same as before):
  matFloor, matWall, matRoof, matGlowCyan, matGlowPink, matGlowPurp
  Plus:
  matGrate: MeshStandardMaterial color 0x111122, wireframe true (for catwalks)
  matGlass: transparent 0.3 opacity, emissive 0x0033ff

Place ALL the following structures:

── OUTER SHELL ──
  Floor: PlaneGeometry(100,100) at y=0 rotated flat, matFloor
  Ceiling: PlaneGeometry(100,100) at y=9 rotated flat, matRoof, backSide
  4 Outer walls 1.5 thick, 9 tall sealing the map

── MAIN HALL (center of map) ──
  Open rectangular space 30x20 units in center
  4 support columns: CylinderGeometry(1, 1, 9, 8) at each corner of hall, matWall
    each column: emissive strip at y=5 (glowing cyan ring)
  Floor: matPlaza (hexagonal texture)
  Overhead lighting: 4 PointLights cyan intensity 3 distance 18

── CORRIDORS A & B (flanking routes) ──
  Left corridor: 8 units wide, 50 units long from north to south wall at x=-25
    Low cover boxes alternating left/right every 12 units: [3,2,3] matWall
    Neon strip lighting along ceiling: 3 PointLights pink
  Right corridor: mirror of left at x=+25

── SERVER ROOM (northwest corner) ──
  Room 18x16 units at (-35, 0, -30)
  Server racks: 6 x BoxGeometry(2, 5, 8) in two rows of 3, matWall
    each rack: cyan emissive horizontal strips at y=1.5, 3.0, 4.5
    PointLight(0x00f5ff, 1, 4) behind each rack row
  Entry doorway: 4-wide gap in south wall
  Window into main hall: PlaneGeometry(8,3) matGlass at y=4

── CONTROL HUB (northeast corner) ──
  Room 18x16 at (+35, 0, -30)
  Central console: BoxGeometry(8,2,4) at y=1, matWall, emissive pink top surface
  Holographic displays: 4 thin planes PlaneGeometry(3,2) standing vertically
    each glowing blue (matGlass) arranged around console
  Raised floor platform: [10,0.4,8] at y=0.4, matGlowCyan

── CATWALK (elevated mid route) ──
  Metal catwalk at y=5.5 crossing north to south through center
  Width: 4 units, matGrate (wireframe)
  Railing each side: [length, 1.2, 0.1] matGlowCyan
  2 ladders connecting ground to catwalk (each side, north-half)
    Ladder: BoxGeometry(0.8, 5.5, 0.2) + rungs every 0.5 units

── CHOKEPOINT (center south) ──
  Narrow 6-unit wide passage between Main Hall and south spawn
  2 concrete barricades [4,1.8,1] each side of choke, matWall
  Overhead: broken ceiling panel hanging at angle (visual only)
  Pink warning light flickering

── SIDE ROOM (southwest) ──
  Room 14x12 at (-35, 0, 30)
  Industrial theme: generator box [6,4,4] matWall center
    Glowing coolant pipes: CylinderGeometry(0.15, 0.15, 3, 6) connecting box to wall, matGlowCyan
  Entry from both corridor and exterior

── GENERATOR ROOM (southeast) ──
  Room 14x12 at (+35, 0, 30)
  Large turbine: CylinderGeometry(3, 3, 5, 16) at center y=2.5, matWall
    Animated: rotate turbine on Y at 0.5 rad/s
  Neon warning tape: alternating yellow/black stripes on floor (canvas texture)
  PointLight(0xff6600, 2, 8) orange — industrial danger light

── SPAWN ZONES ──
  Team A spawns: 5 points across north edge (z=-45, y=1.6, x = -20 -10 0 10 20)
  Team B spawns: 5 points across south edge (z=+45, y=1.6, x = -20 -10 0 10 20)

── LIGHTING ──
  Ambient: AmbientLight(0x060612, 0.8)
  Main hall: 4 x PointLight(0x00f5ff, 3, 20)
  Corridors: 6 x PointLight(0xff2d78, 2, 15) alternating
  Server room: 3 x PointLight(0x00f5ff, 1.5, 8)
  Control hub: 3 x PointLight(0xff2d78, 1.5, 8)
  Generator: PointLight(0xff6600, 2, 10) — FLICKER (random intensity 1.5-3.0)
  Catwalk: 4 x PointLight(0x7b2fff, 1.5, 8) purple under-lighting

── COLLISION ──
  Export getCollisionBoxes() with AABBs for all solid geometry
  Include: outer walls, all room walls, server racks, console, columns, barricades, cover boxes
  Do NOT include: glass, grate, lighting fixtures, decorative elements

ACCEPTANCE CRITERIA:
✓ Map loads with all rooms, corridors, and catwalk visible
✓ Player can walk through all corridors without getting stuck
✓ Player can access catwalk via ladders (teleport trigger when touching ladder)
✓ Neon lighting creates distinct atmosphere in each zone
✓ Generator turbine slowly rotates
✓ Generator light flickers
✓ All collision boxes prevent shooting/walking through solid walls
✓ No floating geometry or Z-fighting
