── SETTINGS MODAL ──

Dark overlay panel, slide in from right:
  AUDIO section:
    Master volume: slider 0-100
    Effects volume: slider 0-100
    Footsteps volume: slider 0-100
  
  CONTROLS section:
    Mouse sensitivity: slider 0.5–3.0, updates in real-time
    ADS sensitivity multiplier: slider 0.3–1.0
    Toggle ADS / Hold ADS: toggle switch
    Invert Y: toggle

  VIDEO section:
    FOV: slider 65–90
    Show FPS: toggle
    Motion blur: toggle (CSS filter on canvas)
  
  All settings saved to localStorage on change.
