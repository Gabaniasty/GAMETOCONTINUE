Build the second map called "OVERWATCH" — an outdoor rooftop environment
with long sightlines perfect for AWP duels, elevated positions, and
multiple levels connected by staircases and ziplines.

── OVERWATCH LAYOUT (100x100 units, outdoor) ──

  Multi-level rooftop district. Exposed to a cyberpunk city skyline.
  3 elevation levels: Ground (y=0), Mid (y=5), High (y=12)

  Ground: open plaza with concrete barriers, 2 building entrances
  Mid: connected rooftops with AC units, vents, cover boxes
  High: 2 sniper nests, 1 center antenna tower

── Create public/js/maps/OverwatchMap.js ──

── SKY & ENVIRONMENT ──
  Skybox: large sphere inside-out, canvas texture:
    Gradient: deep purple-black at top → dark blue at horizon
    City silhouette: irregular skyline of buildings drawn in dark navy at bottom 20% of canvas
    ~300 white pixel stars at random positions
    3 bright "neon billboard" rectangles on distant buildings (glowing pink/cyan rectangles)
    Flying vehicle lights: 4 tiny white dots moving slowly across canvas (animated)
  scene.fog = FogExp2(0x050510, 0.018) — lighter fog for outdoor depth
  Ambient: AmbientLight(0x0a0820, 1.0) — slight purple night sky

── GROUND LEVEL ──
  Main floor: PlaneGeometry(100,100) matFloor
  Central plaza: hexagonal floor texture 40x40 at center, matPlaza

  Concrete barriers (long cover walls):
    8 x BoxGeometry(8, 1.4, 0.8) scattered at ground level in plus pattern around center
    matWall, emissive cyan top-edge trim
  
  2 ground-level buildings (enterable):
    West building: [20, 12, 16] at (-35, 6, 0) matWall
      Interior: hollow box, entry doors north/south side
      Ground floor room: 16x12 with 4 cover crates inside
      Staircase to rooftop: ramp [4, 0.4, 12] rising from y=0 to y=12 inside east wall
    East building: [20, 12, 16] at (+35, 6, 0) matWall
      Mirror layout of West building

  North spawn zone: 5 points z=-45
  South spawn zone: 5 points z=+45

── MID LEVEL (rooftops y=12) ──
  West rooftop slab:  [20, 0.5, 16] at (-35, 12.25, 0) matRoof
  East rooftop slab:  [20, 0.5, 16] at (+35, 12.25, 0) matRoof

  Rooftop details (on each rooftop):
    AC units: 3 x BoxGeometry(2.5, 1.8, 2.5) matWall, emissive pink vent strips
    Ventilation pipes: 2 x CylinderGeometry(0.4, 0.4, 2, 8) matWall with glow tops
    Satellite dish: CylinderGeometry(1.5, 0.1, 0.3, 16) matGlowCyan + thin pole
    Low walls around rooftop edge: [length, 0.9, 0.3] matWall with cyan top trim

  Center bridge (crosses between rooftops at y=12):
    [40, 0.5, 6] at (0, 12.25, 0) matFloor
    2 railing strips each side, matGlowPurp
    3 support arches below: each a BoxGeometry [0.4, 4, 6] dropping to y=8

  North catwalk connecting to building rooftops:
    [6, 0.4, 20] at (-35, 12.25, -25) — extends rooftop toward north spawn
    [6, 0.4, 20] at (+35, 12.25, -25) — east side
  South catwalks mirrored

── HIGH LEVEL — SNIPER NESTS (y=20) ──
  NW nest: [10, 0.5, 10] at (-40, 20, -35) matRoof
    3-sided walls [10, 2.5, 0.5] — open toward map center for sightline
    Sandbags (visual): 3 x BoxGeometry(2, 0.7, 1.2) stacked slightly offset, matWall
    PointLight pink intensity 2 distance 10
    Ladder: trigger at building corner teleports player from rooftop to nest

  SE nest: [10, 0.5, 10] at (+40, 20, +35) matRoof
    Mirror layout, cyan accent

  Center antenna tower at (0, 0, 0):
    Base: BoxGeometry(3, 3, 3) at y=1.5, matWall
    Tower shaft: BoxGeometry(1, 18, 1) at y=10, matWall
    Cross beams: 3 x BoxGeometry(6, 0.2, 0.2) at y=4, 9, 14, matGlowCyan
    Blinking red light: PointLight(0xff0000, 3, 4) at y=20, blinks every 1.2s
    Antenna: CylinderGeometry(0.05, 0.05, 4, 6) at y=22, matGlowPink

── LIGHTING ──
  Ambient: AmbientLight(0x080818, 0.9)
  Street lights: 8 x PointLight(0xaaaaff, 2, 18) on tall poles (BoxGeometry pole + light at top)
    placed evenly around the ground plaza
  Rooftop: PointLight(0x00f5ff, 2.5, 20) and PointLight(0xff2d78, 2.5, 20) on each rooftop
  Bridge: PointLight(0x7b2fff, 2, 12) below center of bridge
  Nest lights: PointLight(0xff2d78, 2, 8) in each sniper nest
  City glow: HemisphereLight(0xff2d78, 0x000008, 0.4) — pink sky, dark ground
  Flying vehicle lights: 4 THREE.PointLight(0xffffff, 1, 3) attached to invisible moving objects
    each follows a random curved path high in the sky, speed 5 units/s

ACCEPTANCE CRITERIA:
✓ City skyline and stars are visible in the background
✓ Both ground-level buildings are enterable
✓ Rooftops are accessible via staircases inside buildings
✓ Center bridge connects east and west rooftops
✓ Sniper nests are accessible via ladders from rooftops
✓ Antenna tower is a visible landmark from all parts of the map
✓ Flying vehicle lights drift slowly across the sky
✓ Long sightlines exist across the bridge and ground plaza (good for AWP)
✓ All collision works — players cannot fall through floors or through buildings
